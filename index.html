About Us


Part of a 1950s stone-built mansion in a prestigious Larnaca area, Sparrow’s Nest provides accommodation with a private garden, barbecue facilities, free WiFi and free private parking. This fully air-conditioned 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom apartment is also equipped with hairdryer, free toiletries, chairs, flat-screen TV. Any special request? Location


Located 3km from Larnaca International Airport, you can reach here within 10 minutes of your arrival and start to explore this beautiful island in no time. Whether you fancy a cold beer at the bar in front of the beach, or a traditional tavern with authentic Meditterian Cuisine, within 20 mins walk you can find anything you fancy at the popular Finikoudes beach. You may also choose a Café next to the Saint Lazarus Church, pop in for a visit to the Byzantine Museum of Saint Lazarus or take some stunning pictures at the Saint Lazarus Square.
